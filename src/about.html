History of my experience

Everything starts from something, so, my story has started from my first mobile phone with camera. I was so excited and started to take pics of everything that stays and moves. A few years ago, I had my new Canon EOS 60d. I was sooo happy and level of my photos grew up also. I've started to learn experience from other photographers by reading their blogs and analyzing their photos. So, I falled in love to spend my evenings with my camera and beautiful sights that you can see below.

But, after years of doing the same things, I was bored, because I can do these type of photos, and I wanted to learn something new. So, I started to take pics of people.

My first experience in this type of photosessions was a session with girl I've never seen efore. I just wrote her a message smth like "Hello, I'm photographer and I want to take shots of people. I have zero experience, but I will do my best". Of course it was a little bit stressful for me, because it was smth new in my life, but the photos turned out really cool, the first time I tried new ideas in processing and I realized that taking photos of people is not so hard and result was also good.
